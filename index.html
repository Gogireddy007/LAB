Welcome to the Steen Lab

Quantitative biogeochemistry and geomicrobiology at the University of Southern California.

Our lab investigates how microbial communities interact with organic matter in marine environments, from the water column to deep sediments. We are actively recruiting passionate researchers.
Our Focus

Research Projects

Extracellular Enzymes

Geochemistry and microbial ecology of extracellular enzymes in aquatic environments

Subsurface Enzymes & Community Evolution

Analyzing enzyme structure in subsurface microbes to understand deep-biosphere community assembly

Bioinformatic Meta-Analyses

Mining public sequence databases to reveal hidden patterns across all microbial life

Thawing Permafrost

Investigating carbon cycling in Arctic ecosystems as permafrost thaws due to climate change

Outreach & Science Communication

Making geomicrobiology accessible and inspiring the next generation of scientists
